Put your mouse here when your turn is over

Rules:

There must be at least 1 square between ships, the ships can't be placed one near the other.

If you hit part of a ship, the outer diagonals will be confirmed empty.

If you break the ship entirely, all space surrounding the ship will be confirmed empty.

If you want to play against computer, leave the second name empty.

In positioning mode, the place you drop the ship is the place where the first 'square' will be placed.
